Program Overview

The 2026 CESASC Scholarship is open for application! Please submit your application by the deadline via the submission link above. Detailed information and Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) of CESASC scholarships are provided at the bottom of this webpage.

CESASC is one of the largest and the most established Chinese-American professional organizations in Southern California. Founded in 1962, CESASC has been dedicated to promoting interests, aspirations, and professional excellence of Chinese-American engineers and scientists. CESASC provides career and educational advancement opportunities, technical exchange, fellowship and community service. In particular, CESASC offers annual scholarships to encourage young students to develop their interests and pursue their careers in the fields of science, engineering, and technology.

In the past ten years, CESASC gave away more than $400,000 in scholarships awarded to more than four hundred excellent students. Many of our scholarship awardees have made great achievements and become leaders in their field. This year, we will continue our tradition and offer 10 general scholarships and 3 Lisa Lu Foundation (盧燕基金會) scholarships for awardees majoring in literature and arts.

Please refer to the FAQ section below for all the necessary details. If you have any further questions, please send email to: cesasc@gmail.com.

All the scholarship recipients will be invited to the CESASC Annual Convention and network with professionals in the fields. Graduate student recipients may also be invited to present their research work at the CESASC Annual Convention.

All awardees need to present their research at the Interdisciplinary Talk (3:00 – 5:30 pm), and attend the CESASC 2026 Convention Gala (starting 6:00 pm) to receive the awards. The awardees are also recommended to join CESASC as members and to attend future CESASC events. Regardless of the results, we encourage all applicants stay involved and participate in future CESASC activities and be part of CESASC family.

Please help us notify your fellow students about this opportunity. We look forward to hearing from all of you.

Thank you for your interest and best of luck in your educational endeavor.

南加州中華科工學會自1962年成立以來，給數百位在美國各大學學習的大學生和研究生頒發過獎學金，目前一些早年的得獎人已經在各自的行業中頗有建樹。該會將繼續給合格的申請人頒發獎學金，現在申請資料已經公布在本學會網上，申請的截止日期是2026年3月13日。

凡是正在美國大學(二年級以上)本科或研究所學習的學生都可以填表申請該獎學金。今年，科工會提供10項科工會獎學金，以及3項盧燕基金會獎學金。有關獎學金申請的各種細節，請進一步參考所附的FAQ文件。若有進一步的問題，請聯系：cesasc@gmail.com

無論得獎與否，期待所有申請人積極參加科工會活動，成為科工會的成員。科工學會獎學金是該學會社區服務的一個重要組成部分，希望學生及其父母借助科工學會的平台，聯係南加州地區從事科學和工程領域的專業人士，發展各自的學業和事業，同時回饋社區。

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What are the history and purpose of the CESASC Scholarship?

Chinese-American Engineers and Scientists Association of Southern California (CESASC), founded in 1962, is a leading Chinese-American professional organization, which promotes and advocates the best interests, aspirations, and professional excellence of Chinese-American engineers and scientists in Southern California. Over the years, CESASC provides annual scholarships for qualified undergraduate and graduate students on the merit and financial need basis. The scholarship funding comes from corporate and individual donors and is managed by the CESASC Scholarship Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. CESASC Scholarship Committee is responsible to select qualified awardees from applicants.

2. Who can apply for the CESASC Scholarship?

Sophomores and above or graduate students in a university or college are eligible. Students from non-Chinese descent and applicants from non-science/engineering majors are also welcomed to apply.

Previous CESASC Scholarship awardees are not eligible for applying the scholarship again.

3. When is the deadline to apply for the CESASC Scholarship?

Applications must be received by 23:59 Pacific Time on March 13, 2026.

4. How do I apply?

You need to submit the application online at http://cesasc.org/scholarship2026

You will need to submit the following supporting materials (preferably in PDF format) in order to have a complete application:

  • A resume of personal experience including publication list (if any) and any notable achievements.
  • Academic transcripts (encouraged while not mandatory).
  • One letter of recommendation from a professor in your field of study (PDF format).

5. How can I find out whether I am awarded with a scholarship/award?

The scholarship awardees will be notified by email before March 23, 2026. Students who do not win the scholarship will also receive notification emails.

6. Are there any other requirements for scholarship awardees?

An interdisciplinary talk will be held in which each awardee will present their research work. Award certificates and checks will be distributed at the convention gala, and awardees must attend the convention to receive their awards.

7. How should I communicate with the CESASC Scholarship committee?

Please send an email to the address: cesasc@gmail.com.
